Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is PulsePro?

PulsePro is a health and wellness technology company specializing in advanced wearable devices for fitness and health monitoring. We provide real-time insights into heart rate, ECG, activity, and more.

How does PulsePro monitor ECG?

PulseProâ€™s wearable devices use sensors to track your heart's electrical activity. Our algorithms then analyze the data to provide real-time ECG readings that you can access instantly through our mobile app.

Are the devices waterproof?

Yes! PulsePro devices are designed to be water-resistant and can withstand daily activities, including washing your hands or light exercise in the rain. However, we recommend avoiding submerging the device in water.

How do I sync my device with the app?

Simply download the PulsePro app from the App Store or Google Play, and follow the on-screen instructions to pair your device via Bluetooth. Once synced, you can monitor your health data in real-time.

What should I do if my PulsePro device isn't working?

If your PulsePro device isn't working, please try resetting the device by holding down the power button for 10 seconds. If the issue persists, contact our support team for further assistance.

Is PulsePro compatible with both iOS and Android devices?

Yes, PulsePro devices are compatible with both iOS and Android devices. You can download the PulsePro app from either the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.

How accurate are the heart rate readings?

PulsePro uses advanced sensors and algorithms to provide accurate heart rate measurements. However, like all wearable devices, the accuracy can vary depending on factors like skin type and placement of the device.

Can I track my sleep with PulsePro?

Yes! PulsePro devices offer sleep tracking features, allowing you to monitor your sleep patterns and get insights into your sleep quality.

What is the battery life of PulsePro devices?

PulsePro devices offer up to 7 days of battery life depending on usage. Charging the device takes around 1-2 hours.

How do I reset my PulsePro device?

To reset your PulsePro device, press and hold the power button for 10 seconds until the device powers off and restarts. This can help resolve minor issues.

Is there a warranty for PulsePro devices?

Yes, PulsePro devices come with a 1-year warranty that covers manufacturing defects. Please refer to our warranty policy for full details.

Can I share my health data with my doctor?

Yes, PulsePro allows you to share your health data with your doctor through the app, providing valuable insights for better healthcare management.

How do I update the PulsePro app?

To update the PulsePro app, visit the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android), and check for any available updates for the app. Install the latest version for improved features and performance.

How can I contact PulsePro support?

You can contact PulsePro support by visiting our support page on the website, or by reaching out to us through the "Contact Us" section in the PulsePro app.
